I am a Master's student in Statistics at Simon Fraser University, with research interests in machine learning and goodness-of-fit. I make use of my strong programming, teamwork, and mathematical skills for social good, and have a passion for promoting science within the community.

I am a recipient of the Alexander Graham Bell Canada Graduate Scholarship (Master's level) of the Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council of Canada (NSERC). I am also a recipient of the Governor General's Silver Medal.
